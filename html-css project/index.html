who are we

ACCOUNTING HOUSE RO KONSULT & LD Ltd develops its activity by performing its accounting services, adhering to the criteria of professional and responsible attitude towards its numerous clients so that they can obtain reliable and timely information that helps the proper management and development of business interests. We can offer you the complete cycle of financial and accounting services of your organization, and you can be sure of the correct and confidential treatment of the information. We strive to be as flexible as possible and to approach each client individually. Our activity is in compliance with the latest requirements of the legal framework regulating the respective business activity and with all requirements in the field of accounting, taxes and insurance.We guarantee quality accounting of our clients and protection of their interests before the respective institutions!
